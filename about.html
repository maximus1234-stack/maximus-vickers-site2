About Me

Founder & CEO of FourMeasure, a self-funded social platform redefining how people rate, review, and discover music. I led the company end-to-end across product, brand, and technical development, building an early user base through direct outreach and interviews. I'm a senior at the University of Miami Herbert Business School pursuing a B.B.A. in Management with a minor in Entrepreneurship. I operate comfortably in diverse environments and actively seek new industries and challenges that expand my perspective. I approach business with intensity and ownership, holding myself to a high standard of execution, efficiency, and continuous growth.
